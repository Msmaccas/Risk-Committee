# Pricing

While RiskCommittee is currently in an early beta release, the following illustrative pricing model outlines how the product could be offered when it matures.  Prices are indicative only and subject to change.  All fees are denominated in USD per month.

| Tier | Intended user | Key features | Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Individual hobbyists and students | Paper‑only portfolio management; limited to 50 proposals per month; access to the basic dashboard and API; community support. | $0 | 
| **Professional** | Solo operators and small funds | Unlimited proposals; advanced rule engine configuration; access to hostile input testing; priority email support; ability to export reports; up to 5 users. | $99 | 
| **Team** | Trading pods and boutique funds | Everything in Professional; team management; custom factor and sector providers; integration with one broker (disabled by default and requires explicit enablement); on‑premise deployment; email and chat support. | $499 | 
| **Enterprise** | Larger funds and regulated entities | Everything in Team; custom SLAs; dedicated account manager; regulatory compliance reports; integration with multiple brokers and data vendors; advanced auditing and approval workflows. | Custom | 

**Important:** RiskCommittee is not a broker, does not execute live trades by default and does not provide investment advice.  Live broker integration is strictly optional and requires regulatory compliance by the user.  The system is designed to be valuable even without connecting a broker.
