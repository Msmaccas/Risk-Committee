## Cross‑Domain Gold Standards

While not directly comparable, the following systems set high standards for robustness, reliability and transparency in trading technology:

* **Backtesting & research platforms** such as **Zipline** and **Backtrader** (Python libraries) provide reproducible backtests and robust data ingestion but lack multi‑agent reasoning or portfolio‑level decision workflows.
* **High‑frequency trading engines** like **NautilusTrader** (above) and proprietary FPGA‑based systems demonstrate deterministic latency management and cross‑venue connectivity.  Their engineering rigor informs our emphasis on deterministic simulation and reproducible results.
* **Market data providers and analytics** (e.g., Bloomberg Terminal, Refinitiv Eikon) offer comprehensive data feeds and analytics but are proprietary and expensive; RiskCommittee must ingest third‑party data via modular providers instead of bundling large data sets.

## Insights for RiskCommittee

1. **Holistic risk governance is missing** – existing multi‑agent frameworks focus on generating and executing trades.  None provide a disciplined portfolio‑level risk committee with clear approval logic and persistent ledgers.  RiskCommittee occupies this niche.
2. **Deterministic, research‑to‑live parity matters** – NautilusTrader shows that research and live systems should share identical execution semantics【196976279006971†L478-L481】.  RiskCommittee will similarly ensure that the paper portfolio and any future live integrations use the same rule engine and data models, avoiding drift.
3. **Typed contracts and gating are essential** – AGENTICAITA introduces typed JSON contracts and gating mechanisms to ensure reproducible, safe execution【893844508922699†L19-L33】.  RiskCommittee will adopt typed schemas and triggers for agent interactions and risk rules.
4. **Human oversight remains crucial** – Robinhood’s agentic trading product enforces manual approvals and push notifications【951197930365073†L33-L41】.  RiskCommittee will always require human sign‑off for live trades and will maintain a paper‑only default mode.
5. **Explanation and audit trails drive trust** – TradingAgents’ persistent decision log【222673196429326†L319-L324】 and AGENTICAITA’s reproducible audit trail【893844508922699†L19-L33】 highlight the need for comprehensive provenance.  RiskCommittee’s ledger and committee memos must be first‑class artifacts.

This benchmark ladder informs the design of RiskCommittee and serves as evidence that a strict, portfolio‑level risk committee product does not currently exist in the market.
